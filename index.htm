E-logbook- Pharmacognosy Division

Pharmacognosy is the study of crude drugs obtained from plants, animals and mineral and their constituents. The word ‘Pharmacognosy’ is derived from the Greek Pharmakon,
‘a drug’ and gignosco, ‘to acquire knowledge’.
